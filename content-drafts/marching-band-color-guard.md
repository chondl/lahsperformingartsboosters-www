## Marching Band & Color Guard

The Marching Band & Color Guard (MBCG) is the most visible — and most ambitious —
program the Boosters support. Each fall, students build a competitive field show from
the ground up and perform it at football games, community events, and Western Band
Association competitions across California. Marching Band & Color Guard also satisfies
the LAHS physical education requirement.

## The season

The year begins with a **fall band camp**, where students learn the show and come
together as a group before classes ramp up. From there the ensemble performs at home
football game halftimes and travels to regional competitions through the fall, building
toward championship weekend. The season is capped by a **senior banquet** celebrating
our graduating performers.

It's an intense, rewarding season — and it only happens with significant parent support.

## Get involved

MBCG depends on parent volunteers more than any other program. Families help with:

- **Equipment trucks** — loading and driving gear to performances and competitions
- **Chaperoning** at camp, games, and overnight competition trips
- **Meals and hospitality** on the road
- **Props, uniforms, and fittings**
- **Volunteer coordination** — leading and scheduling the parent crew

To plug in, **join the Marching Band Google Group**, where schedules and volunteer
sign-ups are posted, and come to the MBCG portion of the monthly Boosters meeting.

## Monthly meeting

The Boosters hold a monthly Zoom meeting open to all parents, with a Marching Band &
Color Guard segment that covers upcoming performances, travel logistics, and volunteer
needs. The link is shared through the Google Group.
