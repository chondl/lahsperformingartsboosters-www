## Instrumental Music

The Instrumental Music program is home to Los Altos High School's concert bands and
orchestras — including Wind Ensemble, Symphonic Band, Chamber and String Orchestras,
and Jazz Band. Students range from first-year players to advanced musicians preparing
honor-ensemble and all-state auditions, and the program continues to grow each year.

## A year of music

The ensembles perform throughout the school year, from fall and winter concerts to a
spring farewell series, often sharing the stage with neighboring schools. Highlights of
a typical year include:

- **Seasonal concerts** in the Eagle Theater and around the community
- **Strictly Strings**, which brings hundreds of elementary string players together with
  our high schoolers — a celebration of the district's music pipeline
- **CMEA festivals** and adjudicated performances that challenge our students
- **Honor and all-state ensembles** for students who audition

The program also keeps evolving, adding new offerings — from jazz to commercial music
production — that open the arts to more students.

## What the Boosters provide

Booster support helps fund instrument repair and supplies, accompanists and clinicians,
festival entries, and the equipment that keeps our growing ensembles sounding their
best. We also help organize **concert refreshments** and hospitality that make
performances feel like a community.
